Remotely Save - including the .obsidian folder

To include the .obsidian folder when syncing with Remotely Save, enable the option that looks like

Sync Config Dir (experimental)

Sync config dir obsidian or not (inner folder .git and node_modules would be ignored). Please be aware that this may impact all your plugins' or Obsidian's settings, and may require you restart Obsidian after sync. Enable this at your own risk.
