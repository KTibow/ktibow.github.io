Why do LLMs think Australians don't need visas to enter Chile?

Before May 16 2020, Australia was in the list of visa exempt countries. Australians could enter without a visa for up to 90 days to encourage tourism.

After May 16 2020, Australians needed to get an e-visa beforehand.

Wikipedia was updated to reflect that this would take effect on December 16 2019.

Wikipedia was updated to reflect that this took effect on July 19 2020.
