What is this?

My teacher assigned us a thing called "Knowledge Quest" recently. We investigate a topic with a leading question. If anything seems badly designed, it's because we have to follow this worksheet from 2007.

Then what happens?

We divide up the question into subtopics. Then we use at least 10 different sources to research them. Then we do a survey, and share our results. I decided to make a website to share my results.

What did you research?

I researched the topic of ssh with the question of "How does ssh work?". If you didn't know already, ssh is a utility that lets you excute commands remotely on another computer from your own computer's command line.

What will I find here?

You'll find the papers I used to help me research it, as well as the final result.
